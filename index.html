Passion for Photography

I first got into photography when I was young. I was at my sister's soccer game when my grandfather handed me his camera. From that game on, I looked forward to going to her games, soley so I could take pictures. As I got older, my passion for photography contiuned to grow, and it brought me to where I am today. In my college life I have taken numerous graduation photos, and even some action shots for Virginia Tech athletics. If college has taught me anything, it has taught me that I should follow my passion. I chose to major in public relations because I fell in love with the person with the camera. I found happiness looking back at the photos and seeign what I captured. Whether it is a home run celebration shot, or a senior popping champagne, there is something about that feeling you get when you see what you captured. I hope I never lose that feeling.
